\Abstract{
	Doozer sticks are a construction material utilized by The Doozers to form the infrastructure of the subterranean world known as Fraggle Rock.  They are primarily constructed of Raddish Dust, a rapidly depleting resource that not only serves as the chief component of doozer sticks, but as the primary food for the beings known as Fraggles.  Fraggles ingest the raddish dust indirectly, by feeding on the completed civil works of the Doozers, forcing the Doozers to re-build their infrastructure over and over again.  The heavy strain put on the raddish dust resource has caused a noticeable decrease in the ease with which the material is mined/grown and fears of a raddish dust shortage have caused mass panic in the Fraggle Rock community.  Here we study the behavioral aspects of the Fraggle to better understand the effects of doozer stick consumption on the radish dust population.
   }
